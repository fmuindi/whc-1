Welcome to The Harv

A house of miracles for every generation

For decades, World Harvest Church has been a place where faith meets power and families find hope, healing, and purpose. Under the leadership of Pastor Rod Parsley, we are a home for world-changers — a house of prayer and healing for the nations.

Our Pastor Our Beliefs

Our Pastor

Pastor Rod Parsley

Pastor Rod Parsley is one of America's most recognized voices for righteousness. His passionate preaching ministry has reached millions around the globe through television, radio, and live events.

He leads World Harvest Church with a vision to be a house of prayer and healing for all nations — bringing hope, breakthrough, and the tangible presence of God each week.
